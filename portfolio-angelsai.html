AngelsAi Flyer Design

Project Year: 2023

Project Background

In 2023, I collaborated with AngelsAi to create a series of flyers promoting their innovative approach to jewelry ownership through tokenization. AngelsAi aims to revolutionize the jewelry market by integrating technology with traditional ownership models.

Project Brief

The objective was to design a set of visually appealing flyers that highlight the future of asset ownership, emphasizing the tokenization concept and its benefits.

Goals
  • Create eye-catching flyers that attract attention and communicate the innovative nature of AngelsAi’s offerings.
  • Clearly present the message about the future of asset ownership and the advantages of tokenization.
  • Include relevant branding and social media information for audience engagement.
Process
  • Research and Inspiration: I analyzed existing designs in the web3 and tokenization sectors to identify effective visual strategies that resonate with the target audience.
  • Content Integration: I ensured each flyer included key messages about the future of asset ownership, along with details about how tokenization works.
  • Visual Design: I employed a modern color palette and dynamic imagery to reflect the innovative spirit of the brand. The typography was selected for clarity and impact.
  • Feedback and Iteration: I presented the initial designs to the AngelsAi team for feedback and made adjustments based on their insights to align with their brand identity.
Unique Solution

The final flyers effectively communicate the innovative concept of tokenization in jewelry ownership, supported by engaging visuals and strong messaging. This design not only promotes AngelsAi’s mission but also strengthens their brand identity in the tech and jewelry industries.

Tools Used
  • Adobe Illustrator: For graphic design and illustration.
  • Adobe Photoshop: For image manipulation and final adjustments.
  • AI Image Generation Tools: For creating compelling visuals.
Experience Gained

This project was an exciting opportunity to help AngelsAi convey their forward-thinking approach to jewelry ownership. I hope the flyers successfully engage the audience and inspire them to explore the future of ownership with AngelsAi
